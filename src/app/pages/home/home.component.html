About Nexus Solutions

Built on a Foundation of Safety & Trust

Nexus Solutions is a Chennai-based specialist in industrial-grade earthing systems, lightning protection, and solar BOS components — delivering compliance-driven solutions to critical infrastructure across India.

Our Mission

Protecting What Matters Most

To deliver world-class earthing and lightning protection solutions that safeguard lives, assets, and infrastructure — engineered for reliability and built to last.

Our Vision

India's Most Trusted Safety Partner

To be the first-choice partner for electrical safety and solar BOS solutions across industrial, commercial, and renewable energy sectors in India.

Our Journey

Founded

Company Established in Chennai

Nexus Solutions was founded with a clear purpose — to bridge the gap between international safety standards and on-ground electrical infrastructure in India. We began with earthing and grounding solutions for industrial clients across Tamil Nadu.

Expansion

Lightning Protection Division Launched

Responding to rising demand from telecom towers, warehouses, and manufacturing facilities, we expanded into ESE lightning arresters and surge protection devices — backed by high-voltage laboratory testing and IS/IEC compliance.

Solar BOS

Solar Balance of System Portfolio Added

With India's solar sector accelerating, Nexus Solutions introduced a complete Solar BOS range — ACDB/DCDB panels, earthing kits, TUV-certified DC cables, and IP65 weatherproof enclosures — serving residential and utility-scale projects.

Today

500+ Projects. Pan-India Presence.

Today, Nexus Solutions serves 500+ customers across infrastructure, renewable energy, and industrial sectors. Every solution is engineered to global standards — IEEE 80, IS 3043, IEC 62305 — and backed by 24/7 dedicated support.
